Click on the Useful Links

  • Baseball & Softball

    South Riverdale Little League

    South Riverdale Little League provides recreational Little League baseball and softball opportunities for all eligible boys and girls of Riverdale and its surrounding communities. Children as young as 4 can join. Games are played in the spring and fall.


  • Cycling

    Hudson River Greenway

    The Hudson River Valley Greenway is a system of parks, trails, kayak/canoe routes, etc. along New York's Hudson River, and the organization that serves to promote and preserve them. The Greenway system includes the Hudson Valley Rail Trail. Fourteen counties are in the Greenway area.


  • Movies in the Park

    KRVC

    Kingsbridge Riverdale Van Cortlandt Development Corporation sponsors movies in the local parks during the summer months (July - Sep). Check their site for the upcoming attractions and locations.


  • Outdoor Swimming Locations

    List of Riverdale Co-ops with Pools
    Van Cortlandt Pool

    There are several co-ops in Riverdale that offer private pool memberships. Van Cortlandt Pool is also nearby.


  • Riverfest

    Riverdale Riverfest

    The Riverfest takes place in June. You can take a sail on an old sailboat or a fire boat. The festival has lots of interesting things to see, including jousting.


  • Soccer

    Riverdale Soccer Club

    The Riverdale Soccer Club organizes boy and girl teams in the spring ad fall. Check out their site for schedules and registration dates.
