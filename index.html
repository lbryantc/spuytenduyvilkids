Spring Activities

Indoor Swim

Indoor swim lessons are popular in preparation for summer pool and beach outings.

Soccer

Youth soccer is very popular. Organized teams play in Spring and Fall.

Baseball

Baseball is available in the Spring and Fall for boys and girls.

Softball

Youth softball is popular in Spring and Fall but only girls are allowed.

Summer Activities

Cycling

From Spuyten Duyvil you can access the Hudson River trails or cross the GWB to NJ.

Sailing

Riverfest takes place in June. You can sail on the Hudson River.

Outdoor Movies

Look for local announcements for the schedule of movies.

Outdoor Swim

There are public pools for use as well as you can purchase private pool memberships in the area.
